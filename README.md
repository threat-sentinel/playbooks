# Threat Sentinel — IR Playbook Library

Public incident response playbooks for common attack scenarios. Written for security teams, IT administrators, consultants and incident responders.

These playbooks are designed for operational use — fast, defensible decisions under pressure. They are intentionally structured for small to mid-sized organizations and do not replace legal advice, forensic expertise, cyber insurance requirements, or organization-specific IR plans.

## Structure

Each playbook follows a consistent six-phase response structure:

1. **Detect & Triage** — Validate, scope, preserve first evidence
2. **Contain** — Stop spread, protect crown-jewel systems
3. **Investigate** — Entry vector, attacker path, persistence, exfiltration
4. **Eradicate** — Remove access, eliminate persistence, close entry vector
5. **Recover** — Restore from trusted states, validate, monitor
6. **Review** — Root cause, lessons learned, control improvements

---

## Usage

- Read the full playbook for your scenario before an incident occurs
- Adapt to your architecture, legal obligations and named responsibilities
- Use the Quick Response Checklist at the bottom of each playbook during an incident
- Decision Models are included to help under pressure
